## 📄 文章
The Federal Reserve is expected to maintain current interest rates in April, according to market概率 analysis.

The CME FedWatch tool shows a 97.4% probability that the Fed will hold rates steady in April, with only a 2.6% chance of a 25-basis-point hike. By June, the probability of holding rates is 92.5%, with just 5% chance of a 25-basis-point cut.

Several factors support the Fed's cautious stance: First, inflation remains above the 2% target. Second, the labor market continues to show strength. Third, geopolitical uncertainties persist.

For global markets, the Fed's policy has significant implications. Maintaining high rates means capital continues flowing to dollar-denominated assets, putting pressure on other currencies. High rates also act as a drag on global economic growth.

Investors should closely monitor Fed communications and economic data releases. The path forward remains uncertain, and markets may experience increased volatility as policy signals become clearer.

## 🔄 二阶效应
Sustained high rates will continue to support the dollar but pressure global growth.

## 🔄 逆向视角
Higher for longer philosophy gives central banks in other countries more policy flexibility.
