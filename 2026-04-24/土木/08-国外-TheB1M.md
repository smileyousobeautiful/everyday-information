## 📄 文章
Digital twin technology is increasingly used in infrastructure management. Virtual replicas of physical infrastructure enable real-time monitoring, simulation, and optimization. Municipalities and asset managers use digital twins to plan maintenance, respond to emergencies, and optimize operations. Integration with IoT sensors and AI algorithms provides actionable insights. Several large-scale digital twin projects demonstrate significant benefits in cost reduction and service improvement. The technology is becoming more accessible as platform costs decrease.
