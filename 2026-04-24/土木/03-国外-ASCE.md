## 📄 文章
American Society of Civil Engineers reports significant progress in sustainable concrete technologies. New formulations using supplementary cementitious materials, carbon capture technologies, and alternative binders are reducing the environmental impact of concrete production. Some new products can achieve up to 70% reduction in carbon emissions compared to traditional Portland cement. Several major construction companies have committed to using low-carbon concrete in their projects. Pilot projects demonstrate that sustainable concrete can meet or exceed performance of traditional concrete in terms of strength and durability.
