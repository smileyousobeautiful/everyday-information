## 📄 文章
ASCE reports growing research investment in infrastructure resilience. Studies focus on designing structures to withstand extreme weather events, sea level rise, and other climate impacts. New design methodologies incorporate climate projections into infrastructure planning. International collaborations are sharing best practices for resilient infrastructure. The research aims to reduce infrastructure damage costs and ensure continuous service during and after extreme events. Recommendations include elevating structures, improving drainage, and using more durable materials.
