## 📄 文章
Wired报道，At CES 2026, Everything Is AI. "Everything is AI now, so nothing is AI," says Anshel Sag, principal analyst at Moor Insights & Strategy. Competing products may use AI capabilities to do all the same things, but the differences that make one win out will be other factors.

## 🔄 二阶效应
AI竞争将从模型能力转向应用创新和用户体验。

## 🔄 逆向视角
过度依赖AI可能导致产品同质化，创新停滞。

## 🎯 情景规划
三个月后：更多AI原生应用将出现。六个月后：差异化创新将显现。一年后：市场格局将稳定。
