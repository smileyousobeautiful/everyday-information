## 📄 Article
The construction cost crisis drives industry transformation. Across major markets, material costs increased 15-25% year-over-year, with labor costs rising 8-12%.

Industry responses include: advanced prefabrication reducing on-site labor dependency, design-build delivery consolidating project risk, and value engineering integrated earlier in design.

Successful projects demonstrate that early contractor involvement, appropriate risk allocation, and technology adoption deliver 10-15% cost savings.

## 🔄 Second Order Effects
