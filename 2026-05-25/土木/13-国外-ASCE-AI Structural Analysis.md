## 📄 Article
AI-powered structural analysis tools are moving from research labs to professional practice. Leading firms report 50x faster design iteration while discovering optimization opportunities missed by traditional methods.

Case studies show: 30% material savings through AI-found optimization patterns, automated detection of potential constructability issues, and rapid evaluation of structural alternatives across complete parameter spaces.

Implementation remains challenging due to liability frameworks and validation requirements. Most firms deploy AI as an "intelligent reviewer" augmenting rather than replacing experienced engineers.
