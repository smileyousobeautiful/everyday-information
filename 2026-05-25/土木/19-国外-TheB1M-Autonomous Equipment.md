## 📄 Article
Autonomous construction equipment reaches practical deployment. Multiple manufacturers offer commercially available autonomous solutions for excavation, grading, and compaction.

Operational benefits demonstrated: 24/7 operation capability, consistent quality regardless of operator fatigue, and elimination of workers from hazardous zones.

Current limitations include: perception system challenges in dynamic environments, site preparation requirements, and supervision/maintenance personnel needs.
