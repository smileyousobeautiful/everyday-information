## 📄 文章
春季过敏季节来临，无数人开始出现鼻塞、流涕、皮肤瘙痒等症状。很多人将原因归结为花粉飘散，但最新的医学研究显示，问题的根源可能在肠道。

人体约70%的免疫细胞分布在肠道及相关淋巴组织中，肠道菌群的平衡直接影响着全身免疫功能。当肠道菌群失调时，肠道的屏障功能受损，一些本不应进入血液的物质会透过肠道进入体内，引发免疫系统的过度反应，从而导致过敏症状的出现。

Experts suggest that during allergy season, in addition to avoiding allergens, attention should also be paid to intestinal health maintenance.

Specific recommendations include: increasing intake of fermented foods such as yogurt and kefir; considering appropriate probiotic supplements; maintaining adequate dietary fiber intake; and ensuring good sleep and stress management. These measures can help improve the gut microbiota balance from the root and build a more robust immune defense.
