## 📄 文章
Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) has been a gold-standard treatment for depression for decades. A new meta-analysis shows the effects of CBT for depression remain strong in 2025.

CBT is a form of psychological treatment that has been demonstrated to be effective for a range of problems including depression, anxiety disorders, alcohol and drug use problems, marital problems, eating disorders, and severe mental illness.

2025 research from Bristol found that mental health literacy is a key factor in how CBT for depression works.
