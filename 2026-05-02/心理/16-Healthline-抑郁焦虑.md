## 📄 文章
Depression and anxiety are common mental health conditions. Treatments include therapy, medications, and lifestyle changes.

Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and SSRIs (selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors) are common treatments.

Meta-analyses confirm that people who exercise regularly experience reduced symptoms of anxiety and depression.

Just 10 minutes of brisk walking can help reduce anxiety and depression symptoms.
