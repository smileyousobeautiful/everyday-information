## 📄 文章
New research from Mount Sinai reveals how meditation induces changes in deep brain areas associated with memory and emotional regulation.

Researchers used intracranial EEG recordings and found that meditation led to changes in activity in the amygdala and hippocampus.

Research shows mindfulness meditation can reduce anxiety and depression, strengthen the immune system, ease pain, and even reshape the brain.

A 2025 Nature study found that meditation may help stimulate the brain's waste removal system, providing restorative benefits like sleep.
