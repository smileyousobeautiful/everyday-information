## 📄 文章
Dartmouth researchers conducted the first-ever clinical trial of a generative AI-powered therapy chatbot and found that the software resulted in significant improvements in participants' symptoms.

The study was published in NEJM AI. People in the study also reported they could trust the AI and felt comfortable discussing mental health concerns.

AI-powered mental health chatbots are now being used to deliver evidence-based interventions, including CBT and DBT, providing ongoing, accessible, and personalized mental health support.

Caution is advised due to risks of inappropriate advice, and human oversight remains crucial.
