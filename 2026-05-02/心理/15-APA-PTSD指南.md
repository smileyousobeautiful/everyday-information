## 📄 文章
A trio of new APA guidelines on treating posttraumatic stress disorder (PTSD) and complex trauma aim to help psychologists broaden their practice.

The guidelines include recommendations for treating adult PTSD and complex trauma, with an emphasis on evidence-based practice.

Key treatments include trauma-focused cognitive behavioral therapy and EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing).

The guidelines aim to help clinicians personalize treatments while still relying on evidence-based practice.
