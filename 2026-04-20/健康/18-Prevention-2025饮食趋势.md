## 📄 文章
Professional nutritionists have identified the major diet trends for 2025:

1. Protein Focus - High-quality protein remains a priority
2. Lab-Grown Meat - Innovative protein sources
3. Plant-Based Options - Sustainable alternatives
4. Mindful Eating - Eating with awareness
5. Gut Health - Microbiome-focused nutrition
6. Functional Foods - Foods with health benefits
7. Personalized Nutrition - Individualized approaches

"Protein remains a priority, with consumers focusing on its role in muscle maintenance, weight management, and overall health," says Scott Keatley, R.D.

Key points:
- Focus on whole foods over processed
- Balance is more important than restriction
- Sustainable approaches work better than fad diets
- Individual needs vary

The focus is shifting from quick fixes to sustainable, balanced nutrition that can be maintained long-term.
