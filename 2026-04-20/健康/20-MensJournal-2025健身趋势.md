## 📄 文章
Biggest Fitness Trends and Predictions of 2025:
1. Building Muscle Reigns Supreme - Strength training remains key
2. Low-Impact Training Meets the Need for Accessible Fitness - For all fitness levels
3. Functional Fitness - Real-world movements
4. Recovery-Focused Training - Rest and regeneration
5. Outdoor Workouts - Nature-based exercise

Building muscle is crucial for overall health, including:
- Metabolic health
- Bone density
- Joint function
- Longevity

Low-impact training options:
- Swimming
- Cycling
- Elliptical
- Yoga
- Pilates

These options reduce joint stress while providing effective workouts.

The trend is toward sustainable, long-term fitness approaches that work for real life, not just gym perfection.
