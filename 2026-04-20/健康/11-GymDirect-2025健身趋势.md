## 📄 文章
Wearable technology tops the fitness trends for 2025, followed by mobile exercise apps and exercise programs for older adults. Other notable trends include holistic mind-body approaches and AI-generated workouts.

According to fitness experts, the biggest trends of 2025 include:
1. Wearable Technology - Smart watches and fitness trackers
2. Mobile Exercise Apps - Home workout solutions
3. Programs for Older Adults - Specialized fitness
4. Mind-Body Approaches - Yoga, Pilates
5. AI-Generated Workouts - Personalized training

Strength training remains important as building muscle is considered crucial for overall health. Low-impact training meets the need for accessible fitness options.

New workout styles gaining popularity:
- Pickleball
- Pilates
- TikTok-inspired workouts
- HYROX
- Rumble Boxing

The 12-3-30 treadmill workout, weighted vests, jiu-jitsu training, and morning jumping routines also went viral in 2025.
