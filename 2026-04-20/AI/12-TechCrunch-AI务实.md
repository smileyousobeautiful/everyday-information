## 📄 文章
In 2026, AI will move from hype to pragmatism. The industry has seen this argument before from French open-weight AI startup Mistral: It argues its small models actually perform better.

The shift from hype to pragmatism means enterprises will focus on AI solutions that deliver measurable results. Companies will prioritize practical applications over impressive but impractical demonstrations.

This transition marks a maturation of the AI industry, where real-world value matters more than theoretical potential.

## 🔄 二阶效应
