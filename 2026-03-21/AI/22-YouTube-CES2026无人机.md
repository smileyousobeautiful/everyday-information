## 📄 文章
CES 2026 has marked a pivotal moment for AI, with "Physical AI" - embodied intelligence in physical devices - emerging as the defining theme of the year.

**The Rise of Physical AI:**

The key signal from CES 2026 is that "Physical AI" has arrived. Unlike the AI that exists primarily in cloud-based applications, physical AI interacts with and operates in the real world. This represents a fundamental shift in how we think about and deploy AI technology.

**AI Drones Take Center Stage:**

AI-powered drones were among the most impressive demonstrations at CES 2026. These aren't just remote-controlled devices - they incorporate advanced AI for navigation, object recognition, decision-making, and autonomous operation.

Key drone innovations include:
- Advanced obstacle avoidance using AI vision systems
- Autonomous flight planning and execution
- Real-time environmental adaptation
- AI-powered surveillance and inspection capabilities

**NVIDIA's Leadership:**

NVIDIA's keynote highlighted the company's vision for physical AI, with new computing platforms designed specifically for robotics and autonomous systems. The DRIVE Hyperion platform represents a new generation of autonomous driving technology.

**Beyond Drones:**

The physical AI revolution extends beyond drones to:
- Autonomous vehicles
- Humanoid robots
- Smart manufacturing systems
- AI-powered healthcare devices

The significance of this shift cannot be overstated. When AI can interact with the physical world, the range of possible applications expands dramatically. From delivery services to emergency response, from agriculture to construction, physical AI promises to transform numerous industries.

This video provides comprehensive coverage of these developments, making it essential viewing for anyone interested in the future of AI technology.
