The "具身智能"(Embodied AI) concept was a major theme, representing the next frontier in AI development. Unlike traditional AI that operates in digital space, embodied AI requires understanding and interacting with the physical world.

This shift has significant implications. As AI systems gain physical presence, they can perform tasks that were previously impossible - from assisting in surgeries to working in hazardous environments.
