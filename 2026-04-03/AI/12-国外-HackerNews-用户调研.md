## ⭐ 值得关注
调研揭示了用户对AI的三大核心期望：(1)可靠性优先于能力；(2)透明度和可解释性；(3)隐私和安全保护。同时用户最担心AI的：错误信息传播、隐私泄露、恶意使用。

## 📝 English Subtitle

## 📄 文章
Anthropic's landmark survey of 81,000 users reveals what people actually want from AI. The key findings show that users prioritize reliability over capability - they want AI that consistently does what it says it will do. Transparency and explainability rank second, with users demanding to understand how AI makes decisions. Privacy and security round out the top concerns, as users worry about data breaches and malicious use of AI systems.

## 🔄 二阶效应
这份调研将影响AI公司的产品优先级，从"更强大"转向"更可靠、更透明"。

## 🔄 逆向视角
