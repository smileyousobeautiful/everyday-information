Iranian missile attacks have caused significant damage to Amazon's AWS data centers in Bahrain and the UAE. Multiple availability zones are now "hard down" - completely offline. This attack comes as US tech giants are investing billions in AI infrastructure across the Middle East. The targeting of cloud computing infrastructure marks a significant escalation in the ongoing conflict.
