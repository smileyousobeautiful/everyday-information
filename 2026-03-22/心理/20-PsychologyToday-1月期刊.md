Why Small Changes Work

Psychology research shows that dramatic changes are often unsustainable. Small, incremental changes are more likely to stick and compound over time. The key is consistency and starting with manageable adjustments.

Getting Started

To implement these changes:
1. Choose one area to focus on
2. Start with the smallest possible change
3. Track your progress
4. Celebrate small wins
5. Gradually add more changes

The power of psychology lies not just in understanding the mind, but in applying that understanding to live better. These small changes can add up to significant improvements in quality of life.
