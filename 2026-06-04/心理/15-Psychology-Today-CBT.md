# Cognitive Behavioral Therapy: Evidence-Based Approach

## 📋 Basic Info
- **Source**: Psychology Today
- **Stage**: Collection/Analysis/Writing/Review

## 💡 Core Insight
CBT is one of the most researched and effective therapies for various mental health conditions.

## 📖 Background
Aaron Beck developed CBT in the 1960s. Over 2,000 studies support its efficacy for depression, anxiety, PTSD, and many other conditions. The core principle: our thoughts affect our feelings, which affect our behaviors—changing any one can change the cycle.
