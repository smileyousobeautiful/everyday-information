# Psychology YouTube - Emotional Intelligence

## 📋 Basic Info
- **Source**: YouTube
- **Stage**: Collection/Analysis/Writing/Review

## 💡 Core Insight
Emotional intelligence can be developed through practice and awareness.

## 📖 Background
Daniel Goleman popularized EQ in 1995. Research shows EQ predicts success better than IQ in many domains. Key components: self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills.
