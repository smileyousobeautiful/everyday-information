# Grief Psychology: Understanding Loss and Bereavement

## 📋 Basic Info
- **Source**: Psychology Today
- **Stage**: Collection/Analysis/Writing/Review

## 💡 Core Insight
Complicated grief affects 7% of bereaved individuals and requires professional intervention.

## 📖 Background
Worden'sfourtasks of mourning remain foundational. Dual Process Model by Stroebe and Schut explains coping oscillation. Elisabeth Kübler-Ross's stages were never meant to be linear. Modern research emphasizes the uniqueness ofeach grief journey.
