## 💡 Core Insight
Cognitive dissonance explains why changing beliefs is so difficult—and how to do it effectively.

## 📖 Background
Leon Festinger introduced cognitive dissonance theory in 1957. Classic experiments showed people change attitudes to match behaviors. The theory explains phenomena from brand loyalty to political polarization. It remains one of social psychology's most influential frameworks.
