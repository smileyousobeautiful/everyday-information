## 📄 文章
How much protein do you need?

General guidelines:
-  sedentary adults: 0.8g per kg body weight
-  active individuals: 1.2-2.0g per kg body weight
-  athletes in training: 1.6-2.2g per kg body weight
-  wanting to build muscle: 1.6-2.2g per kg body weight
-  trying to lose weight: 1.2-1.6g per kg body weight (preserves muscle)

Best protein sources:
-  lean meats, fish, poultry
-  eggs
-  dairy products
-  legumes
-  tofu and soy products
-  protein supplements

Timing matters:
-  Spread protein throughout the day
-  20-40g per meal
-  Post-workout protein within 2 hours
