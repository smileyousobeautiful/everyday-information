## 📄 文章
Types of intermittent fasting:

1. 16:8 method: Fast 16 hours, eat within 8 hours
2. 5:2 diet: Eat normally 5 days, restrict calories 2 days
3. alternate-day fasting: Fast every other day
4. warrior diet: 20-hour fast, 4-hour eating window

Potential benefits:
- Weight loss
- Improved insulin sensitivity
- Cellular cleanup (autophagy)
- Simplified meal planning

Potential risks:
- Overeating during eating windows
- Nutrient deficiencies
- May not suit everyone
- Can cause headaches, fatigue

Not suitable for:
- Pregnant or breastfeeding women
- People with diabetes
- Those with history of eating disorders
- Teenagers

Always consult a healthcare provider before starting any fasting regimen.
