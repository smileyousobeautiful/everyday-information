## 📝 English Subtitle
Five Eyes Warns: AI Cyber Threats Demand Immediate Action

## 📄 文章
Five Eyes urges organizations to "act now" against AI cyber threats.

The intelligence-sharing alliance says that AI models are anticipated to fundamentally transform offensive and defensive cyber capabilities in a matter of months, and that "breaches will occur" as previously unknown vulnerabilities emerge.

"Adversaries are already using AI to move faster and more effectively," said Five Eyes. "Defenders must do the same."

This is the first major joint warning from Five Eyes about AI-powered cyber threats. The warning reflects growing concerns about AI's potential to automate and scale cyber attacks.

Organizations need to invest in AI-powered security tools and develop strategies to counter AI-driven threats.
