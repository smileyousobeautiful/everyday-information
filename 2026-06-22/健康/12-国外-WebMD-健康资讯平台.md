## 📄 文章
WebMD strives to maintain the most comprehensive and reliable source of health and medical information on the internet.

They take their responsibility seriously by:
- Charging content creators to practice journalistic principles of excellence and provide objective, accurate, and balanced reporting
- Maintaining editorial independence and transparency into how they protect the integrity of their content
- Regularly reviewing and updating their content by working with their network of more than 100 doctors and health experts

This commitment to quality has made WebMD one of the most trusted health information sources online.
