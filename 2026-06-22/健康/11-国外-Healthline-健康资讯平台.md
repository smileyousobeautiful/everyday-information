## 📄 文章
Healthline is a leader in digital health publishing for more than 25 years, striving to maintain the most comprehensive and reliable source of health and medical information on the internet.

They recognize the responsibility that comes along with being the most well-known and trusted health information platform — and they take that responsibility seriously by:

1. Charging their content creators to practice journalistic principles of excellence and provide objective, accurate, and balanced reporting
2. Maintaining editorial independence and transparency into how they protect the integrity of their content
3. Regularly reviewing and updating their content by working with their network of more than 100 doctors and health experts

With 130 medical reviewers in their network and 19 years of experience, Healthline serves 50 million monthly readers with medically reviewed content.
