## 📄 文章
In a lengthy blog post outlining YouTube's 2026 plans, CEO Neal Mohan said the company will continue to embrace this new "creative frontier" by soon allowing its creators to throw together Shorts using their AI-generated likeness.

More than a million channels were already using AI tools on YouTube. In 2026, AI will sit right at the centre of how creators make videos, how viewers discover content, and how the platform deals with deepfakes and low-quality uploads.

这意味着YouTube正在全面转型为AI驱动的平台，从内容创作到分发都将由AI主导。
