## 📄 文章
After years of chatbots and image generators, AI is finally leaving the screen. The annual tech showcase in Las Vegas was dominated by "physical AI" and robotics, from Boston Dynamic's newly redesigned Atlas humanoid robot to AI-powered ice makers.

Companion robots and pets were major highlights, showing how AI is moving from virtual to physical.
