## 📄 article
Watch sleep content across platforms and notice repetition: temperature, caffeine timing, light exposure, consistent schedules. These boring fundamentals vastly outperform sleep supplements—which explains why high-engagement content keeps returning to the basics despite their blandness.

Temperature emerges as primary factor. Bedroom temperatures around 65°F/18°C dramatically improve sleep onset and quality. This contradicts intuitive comfort—"feeling cold" seems wrong until understanding what core body temperature must do to initiate sleep.

Temperature protocols work systematically: evening hot bath creates paradoxical cooling through blood vessel dilation, accelerating散热. Sleeping alone enables temperature without partner conflict. Opening windows when tolerable helps more than expected. Cooling mattress pads/blankets provide individualized solutions.

Caffeine deserves more strict handling than typical advice suggests. Half-life of 5-6 hours means afternoon coffee keeps stimulating at bedtime. Zero caffeine after 12pm serves as safe guideline for sensitive individuals. Even those thinking "coffee doesn't affect me" showmeasurable physiological effects masking adenosine rather than neutralizing it.

Morning light exposure anchors circadian rhythms robustly. 10-30 minutes within an hour of waking triggers cortisol awakening surge and appropriate melatonin onset that evening. Light therapy boxes help morning darkness. Timing matters critically—evening light exposure reverses benefits.

Sleep schedule consistency builds sleep pressure. Regular rising times (including weekends!) strengthen homeostatic sleep drive. Sleeping in to "catch up" disrupts this natural accumulation that makes nighttime sleep arrive. Naps before 3pm lasting under 30 minutes provide boost without destroying nighttime sleep.

Wind-down routines prepare neurologically. Blue-light filtering from devices is minimum; complete device avoidance for an hour before bed is optimal. Reading fiction (paper) rather than screens provides acceptable alternative. Gentle stretching or meditation serves transition between day's engagement and night's surrender.

Room darkness matters beyond curtain blackout. Nightlights disorient circadian orientation; bathroom trips during darkness should minimize light exposure. Sleep masks provide consistent darkness regardless of environmental light leaks.
